Reviews

PROORB Visor is not just a product that has been devised as a pandemic solution. Additionally, it qualifies for a wide range of issues like pathogenic smoke, soot, ash, bad odour, car fumes and other pollutants. It is your everyday resource for clean and clear, breathable air.

  • Two-Way Communication

    It comes with a fixed mic and speaker system with two way communication, so that the user can conveniently talk without having to remove the Visor time and again. 

  • Temperature Sensor

    It has a temperature sensor that measures the temperature and displays it on a dual display system.

  • Protection against Heat

    It provides protection from the sun and rigorous heat by using reflective surfaces and air circulation. 

  • Long Battery Life

    It is battery-powered and can be charged efficiently, making it portable and giving it a life that lasts longer. 

  • Deep Penetration Mechanism

    The PROORB Visor features a deep filtration mechanism wherein it filters all particles in the air in a 65-125nm size range—this specifically is the size range of the COVID virus-germs as well. It provides a two-way filtration system. 

  • Impermeable, Breathable Fabric Used

    The fabric used for the neck seal is impermeable fabric which is waterproof, yet breathable. It is manufactured with neoprene lined sheets. 

  • Noise Suppression and Bluetooth

    The Visor comes with a remarkable active noise suppression feature and Bluetooth feature as well.

  • QR Code and Inoculation History of the Wearer

    It displays a QR code, which will be assigned to the wearer and could be linked to our mobile app, so just by scanning it you could access the wearer’s COVID inoculation history.
